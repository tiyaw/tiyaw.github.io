triple canopy symposium 2025

Event Identity Design | Brand collateral | Generative

z

Mar 2025

how might we design an identity that embodies the convergence of physical presence and digital thought for Triple Canopy’s annual symposium?

Triple Canopy, an online art magazine known for its interdisciplinary approach to art, literature, and culture, hosted its annual symposium with a theme centered on the convergence of physical presence and digital thought. For this, I designed an identity that translated these abstract ideas into a cohesive visual language, where glitch effects symbolized transformation, CMYK overlays created layered depth, and pixelated waveforms conveyed digital motion. The result was a dynamic identity system that fused analog texture with pixel precision, embodying the symposium’s spirit of fluidity and dialogue between the physical and digital realms.

Use glitch in positive connotation. Print and digital combo CMYK colors and Gridlite typeface
