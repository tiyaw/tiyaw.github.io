klenvor

Branding | Packaging & Label Design | Digital Ads

May 2024

how can a single brand color become the thread that ties together every touchpoint from packaging to digital ads?

Klenvor is a science-backed beauty and self-care brand. During my internship, I redesigned e-commerce hero images for platforms like Amazon, Myntra and Flipkart, boosting the brand's visibility and sales. Through 10-12 iterations of brand collaterals, I ensured packaging and labels met both the formal and visual requirements of the brand. Across all deliverables, the focus was on making the brand's color an unmistakable part of its identityâ€”whether on a store shelf, a website banner, or in a customer's hands.

The redesigned digital ads significantly boosted product visibility improving Klenvor's product ranking from 1000 to 300 on e-commerce platforms.

behind the scenes

  • 1. analyzing the brand
     Began by understanding Klenvor's current market positioning, tone, and visual identity to identify strengths and areas for refinement
  • 2. competitor analysis in social media static and video content
     Studied social media content of both international and Indian beauty brands. Found that global brands often use Pinterest-style visuals to create desire and aspiration, while Indian brands focus on educational content tailored to specific audiences, such as makeup artists or young women.
  • 3. studying current multicolor packaging trends
     Collected inspiration from skincare and beauty brands to understand how multicolor is used in packaging. Most brands either use two-color combinations, keep an entire product range in one color with other sets in similar-value colors, or use black and white as a base when applying multicolor across different ranges. For Klenvor, we challenged this pattern by pairing the brand's peachy pink with four additional colors, creating a more distinct identity.
