on track

Identity Design | Systems Thinking | Campaign Strategy

May 2025

how can tracking emotions transform from a clinical task into a playful, heartwarming experience?

This project creates a brand that takes on mental health in a warm, human way—starting with measurement-based patient care. Instead of treating surveys and tracking as clinical or overwhelming, the brand turns them into something playful and easy to connect with. Through stickers, stamps, trackers, and simple templates, it helps both individuals and clinicians bring joy, gentleness, and creativity into checking in on well-being. Designed for young adults, the brand makes emotional data something you can actually see, feel, and celebrate—proving that care can be soft, expressive, and genuinely human.
