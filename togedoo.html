togedoo

UI/UX | Social media app

Jun 2025

how might we create a platform for young people to form friendships by sharing or learning something new, fostering memorable experiences through a fun and engaging interface?

Togedoo is a purpose-driven platform designed to help people forge meaningful connections through shared learning and teaching experiences. Users can discover nearby individuals with skills they want to learn or teach, schedule meetups effortlessly, and turn each interaction into a memorable, real-world experience. By combining discovery, skill exchange, and in-app planning, To-Get-To makes building friendships fun, intentional, and effortless.
