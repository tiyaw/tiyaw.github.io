thoughts?

UI/UX | Social media website

Jun 2025

how might we create a social space where writers can effortlessly express their deepest, rawest thoughts while keeping a personal, intimate feel?

Thoughts is a visually fun space where writers can easily read, share, and continue each other’s thoughts without the pressure of spending much effort on beautifying their posts. This platform aims to challenge the conventional social media layouts—the so-called “holy grail of the web”— by breaking away from standard design norms. Additionally, it will offer playful and easy-to-use customization options, allowing writers to add personal touches effortlessly while keeping the experience intuitive and inviting.
