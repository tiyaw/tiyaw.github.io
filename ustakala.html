why does AI not know what is ustakala?

This project started with my curiosity about why AI does not recognize the term "ustakala". I realized that AI models are trained on vast datasets, but they may lack knowledge of niche or culturally specific terms like "ustakala". This project explores ways to bridge that gap by integrating such unique terms into AI training data and models.
