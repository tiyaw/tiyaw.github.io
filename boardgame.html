marketing of board games

UX Research | Systems design thinking

Oct 2024

how might we give potential buyers a feel of the game through interactive elements without requiring them to open the box, especially when the game is unknown?

Extensive UX research was conducted to understand user behavior, preferences, and challenges in purchasing board games, drawing from both primary and secondary sources. Based on these findings, an interactive prototype was developed that uses sequential narrative to guide users through a short promotional video, optional instructions, and a quick 3-minute gameplay demo, followed by a call-to-action. This prototype demonstrates how storytelling and interactivity can enhance customer experience, increase engagement, and support informed purchasing decisions.
